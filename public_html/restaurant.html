Handcrafted, like our food,

Dekalb was constructed from repurposed materials at the hands of our artisan construction force, our materials are decades if not more in age, and required careful planning in order to preserve the effect brought into our present. Our wooden floors were brought from other buildings, regaining purpose again. Pew Benches from Synagogues and Churches, The Windows from a Hospital, and the Bar Top Constructed of old Joists.
